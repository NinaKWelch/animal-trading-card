Sea Otter

Otter grooming includes cheek rubbing and teeth cleaning

  • Scientific Name: Enhydra lutris
  • Average Lenght: 125 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: North Pacific coast

Sea Otters are exellent swimmers and spend their days catching fish and teaching their pups handy tricks, like how to use stones to break open shellfish.
